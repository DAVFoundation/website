What is DAV?

DAV is a computer network that connects self-driving vehicles (such as cars, trucks, and drones) to everyone on that network. Users can pay for the use of any of those vehicles with a token to get a ride somewhere or pick up and deliver a package. People who own those vehicles or the charging stations on the network can make money for the services they provide. This means the average person could have an entire network of self-driving vehicles at their fingertips to bring them anything, or to take them anywhere, with one token.

Why does this need to be on the blockchain?

DAV takes the peer-to-peer (P2P) information transfer capabilities of the internet and the P2P value transfer made possible by blockchain technology and brings them together to their next logical conclusion, enabling P2P transfer of physical things. The blockchain provides for trustless interactions which are critical to a public network owned by everyone.

What is the purpose of the DAV Token?

The DAV token will be used to enable secure transactions between autonomous vehicles, service providers, and users. It establishes a global payment standard between vehicles and their supporting infrastructure, and its smart mission contracts enable trustless cooperation between multiple vehicles and their service providers.

Autonomous Vehicles seem far in the future, how can this be applied today?

DAV is building out the infastructure for all decentralized transportation services. Today our platform can be utilized for what will look like a decentralized Uber, and in the near future the same foundation we've built will be utilized for decentralized autonomous vehicles.

What blockchain will DAV be built on and why?

DAV is built on the Ethereum Blockchain. Ethereum allows for the use of Smart Contracts, which enables trustless cooperation between multiple vehicles and their service providers.
